About Me

Hello! I'm currently a 2nd year studying Computer Science at University of California, Berkeley. If I'm not coding, I'm definitely playing tennis or practicing magic tricks (watch HERE). My favorite part about being a computer scientist is working through a challenging problem, coming up with a solution, and seeing it compile and run!

One of the best projects I've worked on is Caldine. I'm super passionate about mental health, and this web application allows students to make everlasting friendships by eating together at the dinning commons. I think one of the biggest challenges college freshmen face is the social anxiety from eating alone. Caldine aims to break the mentality freshmen have that the reason they are eating alone is because they have no friends, while attempting to combat other mental illnesses such as depression and anxiety at the same time. Feel free to check it out as well as some of my other projects!

Projects

Caldine

Caldine is a social web application that allows students to meet up with other students at the dining common of their choice. With the carrierwave gem, users can even upload a picture of themselves to their profile page as well as other standard information. Never dine alone ever again and make some lasting friendships!
